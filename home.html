BRILLIANT IS THE LEADING IT BRAND OF BD



Brilliant is the leading IT enabled technology brand of Bangladesh. Brilliant Cloud is the first Public cloud service provider of Bangladesh. Along with Data, Cloud services, VAS, PBX, SMS, Internet and last mile connectivity, it also facilitates a broad range of innovative Telephony services like enterprise telephony solution, audio conference bridge, app based PBX, Short code and Toll Free Services.

Brilliant has been in operation since 2013.Brilliant is powered by a group of leading data and telecommunication providers with over a decade of experience. With the combination of Data and Voice licensing portfolio, Brilliant has a diversified range of IT Enabled Services.

Our Concern's : NovoTel, NovoCom, Intercloud Limited, NovoAir.

Brilliant redefines business communications with complete solutions for large and medium enterprises. Brilliant’s quality of services and customer care is the key for the success of many of its customers across the world with 24/7 support center manned with highly skilled telecom engineers.
